Bias Audit
False positive rate disparities across card type, product category, and billing region
⚠ Key Finding
Credit card holders face a 65% higher wrongful block rate than debit card holders (56.8% vs 34.3% FPR, Z=66.6, p<0.001). Transactions classified as ProductCD=0 are flagged at 2.7× the rate of ProductCD=4 (82.3% vs 30.8%). Non-US billing addresses show FPR of 77–85% versus 34.8% for US. These disparities may constitute disparate impact under ECOA and trigger obligations under the EU AI Act (Art. 9–13) and CFPB UDAAP guidance.
